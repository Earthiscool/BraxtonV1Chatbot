Frequent Buyer program in Darts is denoted as FB or FBW


Dog Food
Follow these guidelines for whatever a manufacturer deems is their Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large Size REGARDLESS OF THE BAGS ACTUAL WEIGHT
5 = Small
15 = Medium
25 = Large
40 = Extra Large


Cat Food
Follow these guidelines for whatever a manufacturer deems is their Small and Large Size REGARDLESS OF THE BAGS ACTUAL WEIGHT
5 = Small
15 = Large
