Thought process for deleting or saving email


This is primarily for chad@braxtons.com and cbarone@braxtons.com but can apply to any email or documentation that should be cleaned up occasionally.


* Unsubscribe, if possible, all purely promotional email
   * Keep office or admin related for now, IE: invoices, orders, etc
   * Search and Delete those email en masse
      * In email search use the email extension for the company you want to delete. @company.com select all, delete
   * Delete office or admin related older than 2 years
      * In email search use the email extension for the company you want to delete and sort parameters. @company.com older_than:2y select all, delete
* If the email cannot be “Unsubscribed” because it is a direct email
   * Create a template to ask them to remove us from their list
   * Search and Delete those email en masse
      * In email search use the email extension for the company you want to delete. @company.com select all, delete
* Information from manufacturers and vendors
   * Delete any old information that is no longer relevant from MFGs and Vendors
   * IE: Schedule day changes that have passed.
   * Keep the latest price information from Vendors and MFGs
      * catalog/sheets/PDF/XLS etc
      * MAP information
* Professional in nature
   * Business organizations like MLCC, WBA, Radnor TWP
      * Local events etc (delete past date)
   * MFG or Vendors
      * Recall info
      * Current trade show info (delete past date)
* Trade Magazines
   * Delete older than 6 months (older_than: 6m)
* Create a doc or use this for questions about decisions that are not listed here.


Info on search operators:
You can stack filters with a space in between
older_than:Xy
larger:Xmb
