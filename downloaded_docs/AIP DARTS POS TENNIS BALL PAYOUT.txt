The following instruction is to clearly identify the steps to “pay” a customer for Tennis Balls. Or any other “Paid Out” from the POS drawer.


1. Bring up the customer's account with a pet pass number.
2. Go straight to pay screen and type in a new Misc charge code of “PO”
3. Use a calculator to factor how much to pay out.
   1. We payout 25 cents per tennis ball. (ie. 25 tennis balls = 25 X $0.25 = $7.50)
   2. Input the number into the PayOut amount. Do not make it a negative quantity, it will convert it to negative for you.
4. Input into invoice Message “Tennis Ball PayOut (amount of tennis balls) qty”
5. We only pay out for tennis balls in Cash
6. Conclude transaction through cash and print receipt and store in the blue end of day bin under the register.
7. Take newly received tennis balls to the warehouse associate to be properly in-processed and received into inventory.
