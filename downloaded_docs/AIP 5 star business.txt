Becoming and staying a Five Star Business


Philosophy/Thoughts:


The difference between a 4 star and a 5 Star business may seem insignificant but it is the nuanced level of detail that is exactly what makes the difference. 


Word of mouth advertising and referrals are the best way to build a business. Often that sets high expectations in the new visitor’s mind. If we can exceed those expectations and maybe even wow them with their experience here, we will have a customer for a long time and probably someone who is happy referring us to their peers.
