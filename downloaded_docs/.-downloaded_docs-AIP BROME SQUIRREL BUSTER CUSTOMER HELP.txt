Brome Feeder Replacement Parts


This document is a step by step guide on how to assist a customer with a broken part on their squirrel buster feeder. We can’t warranty these for the customer directly through Braxton’s, but we can assist them in a very easy process. This process should only take 5 minutes.


1. With customer in front of you, visit https://bromebirdcare.com/customersupport/ 
   1. May have changed to https://bromebirdcare.com/en/contact/replacement-parts/
2. Select the feeder (the “next” button is hidden in the bottom left corner).
3. Use the part diagram button to reference the correct part number.
4. Input part number and a brief description of the problem (no picture necessary).
5. On the next page, fill out all of the needed information with the customer’s information (DO NOT USE BRAXTON’S INFORMATION).
6. Then Submit, Inform the customer Brome will be in touch with them for a replacement part or feeder and if they need any assistance at all with fixing it please come back to the store and we will assist them.
