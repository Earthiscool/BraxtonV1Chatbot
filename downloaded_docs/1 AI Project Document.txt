Project collaboration between Braxton’s Animal Works and Apex Intelligent Systems


Purpose/Objective:
* To create an AI driven bot for the internal operating systems of Braxton’s Animal Works.
   * The bot will use SOPs and process documents supplied by BAW in a shared Google Drive 
   * Documents may be used by the bot for training and to answer any questions about the operations of the company
   * Develop a system to create additional bots for other purposes


Ideal outcome:
* Work based on lean method for small deliverables that can be tested for feedback and modified before spending too much time
* Voice activated bot that can answer in text or voice
* Accurate responses to the questions based on the information within the documentation
* HITL training for continuous improvement of the bot’s output and accuracy
* Available through a private URL or internal WIKI page via browser


How project is carried out:
* Co-ordinate with AIS to build AI chatbot
* Braxton’s will load all SOP and process documentation into the Google Drive shared project folder
