Darts Home Delivery System


To Input a Subscription
Enter customer’s pet pass into darts pos
Click the add/alter subscriptions button
Create new order sheet by entering the following information:
Description: Delivery (Other examples: Pick-Up or Delivery-2 if they have different delivery rotations)
Next Delivery Date
Day of Week: TUE or THU
Days between Deliveries: 28=4 Weeks 20=3 Weeks 13=2 Weeks 7=1 Week
Pick Up?: Check if it is an in-store pick up
Enter PLU’s of recurring items
Enter Recurring Delivery Charge
Save


Process Deliveries
From Darts POS/Invoice Processing
[Home Delivery System]
Batch Process Deliveries to Hold
Start Date today’s date through Tuesday’s date for Tuesday Deliveries, or Wednesday through Thursday for Thursday Deliveries
As of Now, Do not send email notice
Process Orders


From the POS screen
Recall holds
Call customers to confirm order and delivery
Process delivery
Print Pick Ticket if awaiting special order or a delivery for a product
Give pick ticket to warehouse so they know what to pull
